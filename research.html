Research

I'm a PhD student at the University of British Columbia (UBC). I am interested in making robot do dynamic movement using tools from control theory and reinforcement learning. My Ph.D. is supervised by Prof. Michiel van de Panne in the Imager Lab of UBC. I did my master at Georgia Tech, supervised by Prof. Karen Liu.

Fields of interest:

  • Reinforcement Learning
  • Control Theory
  • Legged Robots
  • Computer Animation
